BioLineRx is a pre-commercial-stage biopharmaceutical company advancing best-in-class therapies for people living with cancer and rare disease. Headquartered in Israel with a growing global commercial presence, BioLineRx powers the process of developing impactful drugs with end-to-end expertise in drug development and commercialization, ensuring life-changing discoveries move beyond the bench to the bedside.

Our lead asset, motixafortide, is a best-in-class, high-affinity CXCR4 antagonist currently in the FDA approval process for mobilization of stem cells for transplantation in multiple myeloma patients as well as undergoing clinical evaluation for use in other indications including pancreatic cancer and mobilization for gene therapy in sickle cell disease patients.
